# 🏅 What Are Skill Badges?

Skill Badges are **achievement-based credentials** available on the **Google Cloud Skills platform** that demonstrate your practical knowledge of specific Google Cloud technologies.

Each Skill Badge consists of a **set of guided hands-on labs** designed to test your understanding of cloud concepts and services.

To earn a Skill Badge:

• You must complete **all the labs included in the badge**  
• Each lab involves performing **real tasks using Google Cloud services**  
• After successfully finishing every lab, the **Skill Badge is awarded**

# 🎯 Skill Badge Requirement

To earn a Skill Badge you must:

✔ Complete **every lab included in the badge**  
✔ Successfully finish all required tasks inside each lab  
✔ Ensure the lab progress shows **completed status**

Once all labs are completed, the **Skill Badge will automatically appear on your profile.**

---

# 📊 Skill Badges & Arcade Points

In the **Google Cloud Arcade Program**, Skill Badges contribute to your total Arcade points.

The general rule is:

**🏅 2 Skill Badges = 1 Arcade Point**

Example:

| Skill Badges Completed | Points Earned |
|------------------------|--------------|
| 2 Skill Badges | 1 Point |
| 10 Skill Badges | 5 Points |
| 20 Skill Badges | 10 Points |

Completing more Skill Badges increases your chances of reaching **higher reward tiers in the Arcade program.**
