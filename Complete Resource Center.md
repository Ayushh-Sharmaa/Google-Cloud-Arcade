## 📚 Google Cloud Arcade – Complete Resource Center

This section provides a curated collection of important resources, official links, guides, and support contacts to help learners navigate the **Google Cloud Arcade Program** effectively.  
Each resource listed below plays a specific role in helping participants understand the program, complete labs, earn skill badges, and track their progress.

---

# 🌐 Official Arcade Platforms

## 🎮 Arcade Website
The **Google Cloud Arcade portal** is the main platform where all Arcade games, learning paths, and challenges are hosted. Participants can explore available games, complete labs, earn points, and unlock rewards through this portal.

🔗 https://go.cloudskillsboost.google/arcade

---

## 🧑‍🏫 Arcade Facilitator Program
The **Arcade Facilitator Program** allows selected community leaders and mentors to guide participants through the learning journey. Facilitators organize study sessions, provide guidance, and help learners understand Google Cloud technologies.
